CYCLONE VARUNA SET TO HIT EASTERN COAST TOMMOROW

The Indian Meteorological Department (IMD) has issued a red alert for parts of Andhra Pradesh and Odisha as Cyclone Varuna moves closer to the eastern coastline. Winds up to 130 km/h and heavy rainfall are expected. Residents have been advised to move to safe shelters, and fishermen are warned to stay ashore. Over 1,000 relief camps have been set up in vulnerable areas.

SPORTS

Gukesh Dommaraju (born 29 May 2006) is an Indian chess grandmaster and the reigning World Chess Champion. A chess prodigy, Gukesh is the youngest undisputed[a] world champion, the youngest player to have surpassed a FIDE rating of 2750, doing so at the age of 17, and the third-youngest to have surpassed 2700 Elo at the age of 16. He earned the title of grandmaster at the age of 12 and isthe third-youngest grandmaster in chess history.His parents, both doctors, made significant sacrifices to support his chess career, with his mother becoming the sole breadwinner while his father traveled with him. His coach, Vishnu Prasanna, encouraged him to not use chess engines (a tool used by many players) until he reached a certain rating, which helped him develop strong calculation and tactical skills, according to The Indian Express.

KOLLI HILLS

Kolli Hills (Kollimalai) offers a variety of attractions for nature lovers and those seeking a spiritual experience. Key places to visit include the Agaya Gangai Waterfalls, the Arapaleeswarar Temple, and the Siddhar Caves. Other notable spots are the Masila Falls, the Botanical Garden, and various viewpoints like Seekupaarai.Kolli Hills, also known as Kollimalai, is a hill station in Tamil Nadu, India, known for its natural beauty and waterfalls, including Agaya Gangai. The distance from Namakkal town is about 45 km. Agaya Gangai waterfall is a major attraction, a 300-foot cascade formed by the Aiyaru river. It's accessible by descending 1196 steps. Other notable spots include Siddhar Caves, viewpoints, and temples like Arapaleeswarar Temple.
